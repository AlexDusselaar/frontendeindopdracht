Welcome!

My name is Alex, better knows as 'CosAlex', and I'm a competitive cosplayer and content creator from the Netherlands. I've been doing cosplay since 2019 and I started creating content on social media in 2021. As of right now I'm well known with 10,6k followers on TikTok and 700 on Instagram. Most people in the cosplay community know me as 'that one Rosalina cosplayer' because of my performance as Rosalina from Super Mario Galaxy at Heroes Made in Asia 2023. However, before this I was better known from my catwalk as Kagamine Len at Dutch Comic Con 2022, since the video got a lot of views on both TikTok and Instagram.
